Welcome To The Chalkboard

Initiate | Invent | Inspire

The ChalkBoard was started with an aim to provide quality education in the best possible environment for children. We focus on making learning fun where children can gain knowledge by practical and experimental approach to education. Although we are a new establishment, we are determined to provide nothing but the best to the students.

Under the able guidance of esteemed and qualified founders and staff members the students are guaranteed to bloom and prosper

 

Aim

We at The ChalkBoard believe that each child is born with a potential to make the world a better, brighter place and to prosper with it. We understand that the preparatory age is extremely crucial as this is the time when the foundation is laid, where a child acquires traits and skills to achieve his/her goals. Children are like a clean chalk-board and only the right form of education can help them create an environment that is beneficial to them as well as to the entire community at large.
