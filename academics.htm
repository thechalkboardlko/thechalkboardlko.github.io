CURRICULUM

The school follows a comprehensive syllabus that implements play-way method of teaching. With a generation that is exposed to all form of technology it is imperative that the methodology should be regularly updated and made creative so that it could engage and stimulate children mentally.

Therefore, our curriculum has been designed to help children to grow physically, socially, emotionally and cognitively. The school insists on classroom programmes to be highly interactive enabling students to benefit maximum from the lessons.

ACTIVITIES

Apart from the textbooks, activities are an effective and engaging method to educate a child about various aspects of academics, society, family and peer groups. Studies have shown that lessons taught through activities and worksheets help children grasp the concepts faster as well as retain and recall them effectively. Innovative worksheets have been found to play a crucial role in the way a child learns inside classroom. We also stress on including special activities that help children develop their motor and coordination skills as well, gaining more confidence each day.
